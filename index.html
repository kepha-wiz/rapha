Our Story

Rapha Orphans International was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide hope and healing to the most vulnerable children in Jinja, Uganda. What started as a small outreach program has grown into a comprehensive support system that addresses education, healthcare, nutrition, and emotional well-being.

Our name "Rapha" comes from the Hebrew word meaning "to heal" - reflecting our commitment to healing not just physical needs, but also the emotional and spiritual wounds that orphaned and vulnerable children often carry.

Today, we operate multiple programs serving hundreds of children and their caregivers, creating sustainable change that ripples through entire communities.

Our Programs

We take a holistic approach to child welfare, addressing multiple needs to ensure each child has the opportunity to thrive.

Education Initiative

We provide school fees, uniforms, books, and tutoring to ensure every child has access to quality education. Our after-school programs offer additional support in core subjects and life skills.

Support Education

Healthcare Access

Through partnerships with local clinics, we ensure all children receive regular check-ups, vaccinations, and prompt treatment for illnesses. Our health education program teaches preventive practices.

Support Healthcare

Nutrition Support

We provide daily nutritious meals and food packages for families facing food insecurity. Our school feeding program ensures children have the energy they need to learn and grow.

Caregiver Empowerment

We equip guardians with vocational skills, business training, and micro-loans to create sustainable income sources, strengthening families and communities.
